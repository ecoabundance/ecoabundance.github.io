Cats are often misunderstood. It is not as easy to understand them as it is dogs, given their limited facial muscles.

Many simply give up, making assumptions based on that misunderstanding.

Just as we learn to read the body language cues of dogs, we can also learn to read the body language cues of cats.

It's easier than you think!
